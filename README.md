# Real Estate Market Intelligence – Jabodetabek

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes the residential property market in Jabodetabek (Jakarta, Tangerang, Depok, Bogor, Bekasi) to identify pricing structure, market segmentation, and investment characteristics.

The objective is to uncover macro-level insights and understand how each city positions itself within the broader regional market.

## 🧠 Key Market Segmentation

The Jabodetabek market is **not homogeneous** and can be segmented into four structural layers:

### 1️⃣ Core Premium Market → Jakarta
- Highest pricing level
- Premium urban positioning
- Supply relatively controlled

### 2️⃣ Premium Expansion → Tangerang
- Strong pricing close to Jakarta
- Comparable volume
- Emerging high-end residential growth

### 3️⃣ Mass & Volume Market → Bogor & Bekasi
- Lower entry price
- Higher transaction volume (especially Bogor)
- Middle-income driven demand

### 4️⃣ Growth Transition → Depok
- Positioned between premium and mass market
- Moderate price and volume
- Potential long-term appreciation zone

---

## 📈 Final Insight

Price per m² decreases consistently as we move away from Jakarta, while listing volume does not always follow price level. 

This indicates:
- Jakarta drives pricing benchmark.
- Bogor shows strong absorption despite lower price.
- Depok presents transitional growth opportunity.
- Tangerang competes directly with Jakarta in premium expansion.

The region demonstrates a clear **concentric economic pricing structure** centered on Jakarta.
